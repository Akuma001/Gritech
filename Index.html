WHO WE ARE

Gritech Global Services is an organization with its business interest, spanning Architectural drawing, Building maintenance, Solar installation, Building construction, paint production, supply and application.
Gritech Global Services (GGS) is an organization registered in accordance with Corporate Affairs Commission in Nigeria. The enterprise commenced operation in January, 2004 providing the following services - IT Services, Civil Works, Solar Panel Installation and General Supply of Goods and Services. We have, over the years committed to excellence through systematic and disciplined Management operations, gained the trust of our clients and hold ourselves to the highest ethical standards and behave in ways that earn the trust of our clients. Gritech Global Services is determined to and committed to improving our business for an enduring legacy.
